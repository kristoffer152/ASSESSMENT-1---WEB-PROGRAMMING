CAST AND CREATIVE TEAM OF NARUTO


AUTHOR/CREATOR

  • Masashi Kishimoto - The creator, author, and illustrator of the original Naruto manga.
  • Mikio Ikemoto - was a chief assistant to Kishimoto during the original run (who now illustrates Boruto).
  • Kenji Taira - created the spin-off comedy series Rock Lee & His Ninja Pals and Uchiha Sasuke no Sharingan Den.

ANIME PRODUCTION STAFF

  • Hayato Date - Director
  • Studio Pierrot - Studio
  • TV Tokyo - Original Network
