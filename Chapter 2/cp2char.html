DATTEBAYO

MAIN CHARACTERS

  • Naruto Uzumaki

    The protagonist, known for his perseverance, strong will, and determination. He wants to be the Hokage and is often seen as a troublemaker, but his heart is pure.

  • Sasuke Uchiha

    One of Naruto's closest friends and rivals. Sasuke is a genius ninja, but he's driven by revenge after the massacre of his clan by his older brother, Itachi Uchiha.

  • Sakura Haruno

    Naruto’s teammate and childhood friend. Initially lacking confidence, she grows to be a powerful medical ninja and one of the strongest characters in the series.

  • Kakashi Hatake

    The leader of Team 7, Naruto’s mentor, and a highly skilled ninja. He has a mysterious past, including the loss of his closest friends.

SECONDARY CHARACTERS

  • Minato Namikaze - Naruto's Father

    Minato was a genius ninja who became Hokage at a very young age. He was calm, intelligent, and always thinking several steps ahead in battle. Unlike many powerful shinobi, he was kind and humble.

  • Kushina Uzumaki - Naruto's Mother

    Kushina represents strength, sacrifice, and unconditional love. Her story is not only important to Naruto’s origins but also to the deeper themes of the series, including resilience, family, and the power of love over hatred.

  • Jiraiya

    is a legendary ninja of Konohagakure, one of the Legendary Sannin, and mentor to Naruto Uzumaki. Known for his boisterous, perverted, and adventurous personality, Jiraiya is also a wise and powerful shinobi, skilled in Sage Mode, summoning toads, ninjutsu, and sealing techniques. He taught Naruto the Rasengan and trained him in controlling chakra.

  • Tsunade

    is a legendary ninja of Konohagakure, one of the Legendary Sannin, and the Fifth Hokage. Renowned as the world’s strongest female shinobi, she is a master of medical ninjutsu, superhuman strength, and chakra-enhanced combat.

TEAM 8

  • Hinata Hyuga

    A shy but kind ninja from the Hyuga clan. She deeply admires Naruto and gradually becomes stronger and more confident.

  • Kiba Inuzuka

    Kiba Inuzuka is a member of Team 8 from the Inuzuka Clan, a clan known for their canine-like traits and partnership with ninja dogs (ninken). Each clan member fights alongside a loyal dog companion.

  • Akamaru

    Akamaru is Kiba Inuzuka’s ninja dog, best friend, and constant companion. Early in the series, Kiba often carries Akamaru on his head or inside his jacket.

  • Shino Aburame

    Shino Aburame is a member of Team 8 from the Aburame Clan, a ninja family that forms a symbiotic relationship with a special breed of insects called destruction bugs (kikaichū). These insects live inside Shino’s body and can be directed in battle to surround opponents and absorb their chakra.

  • Kurenai Yuhi

    Kurenai Yuhi is the leader of Team 8 and a highly skilled genjutsu user, specializing in illusions involving plants. She acts as a mentor and surrogate parent to her students, particularly Hinata Hyuga, helping her overcome personal weaknesses.

TEAM 10 (INO-SHIKA-CHO)

  • Shikamaru Nara

    A genius-level strategist who prefers a lazy lifestyle. Uses shadow-manipulation techniques and later becomes a trusted advisor.

  • Choji Akimichi

    Choji Akimichi is a member of Team 10, known for his love of food. Despite his portly appearance, he insists he is “big-boned” rather than fat. If someone calls him fat, Choji becomes defensive and fights with increased determination, even defeating powerful enemies like members of the Sound Four.

  • Ino Yamanaka

    Ino Yamanaka is a member of Team 10 and heir to the Yamanaka Clan, known for her fashion sense and popularity in the ninja academy. She is the only daughter of Inoichi Yamanaka. Ino was a childhood friend of Sakura Haruno, helping her develop confidence, though their friendship was temporarily strained when they both liked Sasuke Uchiha. They later reconcile while maintaining a friendly rivalry.

  • Shino Aburame

    Shino Aburame is a member of Team 8 from the Aburame Clan, a ninja family that forms a symbiotic relationship with a special breed of insects called destruction bugs (kikaichū). These insects live inside Shino’s body and can be directed in battle to surround opponents and absorb their chakra.

  • Asuma Sarutobi

    Asuma Sarutobi is the leader of Team 10 and a skilled ninja from the Sarutobi Clan. He is the son of the Third Hokage, Hiruzen Sarutobi, grandson of Sasuke Sarutobi, and uncle of Konohamaru Sarutobi.

TEAM 9 (TEAM GUY)

  • Rock Lee

    Rock Lee is a member of Team Guy and the favorite student of Might Guy. Unlike most ninja, Lee cannot use ninjutsu or genjutsu, but his unwavering determination and dedication to taijutsu (hand-to-hand combat) impresses his teacher.

  • Neji Hyuga

    Neji Hyuga is a member of Team Guy, a child prodigy of the Hyuga Clan, and Hinata Hyuga’s cousin. Initially, as part of the branch family, Neji believed in a fatalistic philosophy, thinking that one’s destiny is inescapable and that the weak are doomed to remain weak.

  • Tenten

    Tenten is a member of Team Guy, specializing in weaponry. She is skilled with a wide range of tools, including projectiles, close-combat weapons, and explosives, making her a versatile fighter. Tenten also assists Neji Hyuga in training his defensive techniques, respecting his ability to block her attacks consistently.

  • Might Guy

    Might Guy is the leader of Team Guy and a master of taijutsu, dedicating much of his time to training his pupil, Rock Lee, who models himself on Guy’s ideals and philosophy. Guy follows a “My Rules” approach, imposing strict self-discipline and penalties to grow stronger. Unlike Lee, Guy can also use ninjutsu and genjutsu, though he primarily relies on physical combat.

SAND SIBLINGS

  • Baki

    Baki is the Sand Siblings’ instructor and a member of Sunagakure’s advisory council. He is a skilled and experienced ninja who played a notable role during the Konoha Crush, where he killed Gekko Hayate.

  • Gaara

    Gaara is the youngest of the Sand Siblings and serves as a foil to Naruto Uzumaki, sharing a similar traumatic childhood but developing a very different personality. Before his birth, Gaara was made into a weapon by his father by becoming the Jinchūriki of the One-Tail, Shukaku. This caused the death of his mother during childbirth, and he was placed under the care of his maternal uncle.

  • Kankuro

    Kankuro is the middle child of the Sand Siblings. Early in the series, he is often at odds with Gaara, though he holds his tongue out of fear. In Part II, as Gaara shares his dreams and motivations, Kankuro becomes fiercely protective of his brother, willing to risk his life to save him when kidnapped.

  • Temari

    Temari is the oldest of the Sand Siblings. Unlike her more combat-focused brothers, she values peace and often questions Sunagakure’s decisions to go to war with Konohagakure. This outlook continues in Part II, where she acts as a liaison between the two villages to promote cooperation.

VILLAINS / ANTAGONIST

  • Zabuza Momochi

    Zabuza Momochi is a former member of Kirigakure’s Seven Swordsmen of the Mist, a group of elite ninja known for wielding large swords. He is one of Team Seven’s first opponents and wields the massive sword Kubikiri Bōchō (Decapitating Knife).

  • Haku

    Haku is the most loyal follower of Zabuza Momochi. Orphaned at a young age, Haku’s father killed his mother after discovering Haku possessed the rare genetic ability Hyōton (Ice Release), which combines wind and water elements to create ice.

  • Orochimaru

    Orochimaru is an S-ranked criminal and the primary antagonist of Part I of Naruto. Formerly a ninja of Konohagakure, he trained under Hiruzen Sarutobi alongside Jiraiya and Tsunade, together known as the Legendary Sannin.

  • Kabuto Yakushi

    Kabuto Yakushi is a skilled spy and Orochimaru’s right-hand operative, though he often acts according to his own ambitions. As a child with no memory of his past, he was found on a battlefield by a nun from Konohagakure and later raised by her. He was recruited by the Foundation as a spy, infiltrating multiple countries and organizations. Labeled a risk for knowing too many secrets, Kabuto joins Orochimaru after being promised a true identity.

AKATSUKI

    The Akatsuki is a criminal organization of rogue ninja that serves as the primary antagonistic force in Part II of Naruto. The group was originally founded by Yahiko, Nagato, and Konan. After Yahiko’s death, leadership passed to Nagato (Pain), with Obito Uchiha acting as his secret benefactor. Akatsuki's goal is world peace through the power of the nine Tailed Beasts, with Nagato assuming it would involve mass destruction giving way to a world without war. To achieve their goal of collecting all nine Tailed Beasts, the Akatsuki recruited several rogue ninja, including Deidara, Sasori, and Hidan, among other notable Akatsuki members, to join their ranks

  • Obito Uchiha

    Obito Uchiha, also known by the alias Tobi, is the true leader of the Akatsuki and the primary antagonist for most of Part II of Naruto.

  • Zetsu

    Zetsu is the only nonhuman member of the Akatsuki and serves as Obito’s right-hand operative. He is unique in being without a human partner and operates as a spy, saboteur, and manipulator within the organization.

  • Kisame Hoshigaki

    Kisame Hoshigaki is a former ninja of Kirigakure and a member of the Akatsuki, partnered with Itachi Uchiha. He is known for his shark-like appearance and immense chakra reserves.

  • Konan

    Konan is the only female member of the Akatsuki, known for her calm, strategic mind and deadly origami-based combat techniques. She is also Pain’s partner and a former student of Jiraiya.

  • Nagato

    Nagato, known in the series primarily by his alias Pain, is the founder and recognized leader of the Akatsuki. He works closely with Konan, while Obito Uchiha secretly acts as his benefactor and manipulator.

  • Itachi Uchiha

    Itachi Uchiha is a former ninja of Konohagakure, older brother to Sasuke Uchiha, and partnered with Kisame Hoshigaki during his time in the Akatsuki. He is one of the most complex characters in the series, transitioning from being seen as a villain to a tragic hero.

  • Deidara

    Deidara is a former ninja of Iwagakure, who became a terrorist bomber-for-hire before joining the Akatsuki under Itachi Uchiha’s directive. He is known for his artistic philosophy that “art is an explosion” and his obsession with proving his creations superior to others.

  • Hidan

    Hidan is the immortal, sadomasochistic ninja from Yugakure (the Village Hidden in Boiling Water) and a member of Akatsuki, partnered with Kakuzu. He is notorious for his foul-mouthed personality, bloodlust, and extreme religious devotion.

  • Kakuzu

    Kakuzu is a former ninja of Takigakure (the Village Hidden in the Waterfalls) and the partner of Hidan in Akatsuki. Unlike Hidan, Kakuzu is motivated primarily by greed and personal gain, making him one of the most calculating and dangerous members of the organization.

  • Sasori

    Sasori was a former ninja of Sunagakure (the Hidden Sand Village) and a member of the Akatsuki, partnered with Deidara. He was renowned as a puppet master with a deadly talent for turning humans into puppets and creating almost indestructible weapons.

  • Madara Uchiha

    Madara Uchiha is one of the co-founders of Konohagakure (the Hidden Leaf Village), alongside Hashirama Senju, and a central figure in the Naruto series’ overarching conflict. He is also a key architect behind the Eye of the Moon Plan.
